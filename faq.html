FAQ's

Q1:What is AEMS?

ANS:AEMS means Automated Employee Management System.

Q2:What’s the purpose of AEMS?

ANS:The main purpose of AEMS is to simplify employee management more secure and friendly with sufficient privileges that will help a company to maintain the database of employees easily.

Q3:What’s the process about login?

ANS:An employee will be provided with a password and username after joining.

Q4:What’s the payment option?

ANS:An employee have a pay scale according to their rank , their salary will increase by following their pay scale .In this option employee can check the data about his payment.

Q5:How the leave request option work?

ANS:After an employee fill up the leave request form with proper cause of his leave , it will be verified by the proper authority , then he\she will be notified if that is granted or not .
